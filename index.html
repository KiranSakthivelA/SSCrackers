Stay Safe

Safety Tips for Crackers

Celebrate responsibly – follow these important safety guidelines

Do's
  • Always burst crackers in open areas
  • Keep a bucket of water or sand nearby
  • Read instructions before use
  • Keep children under adult supervision
  • Wear cotton clothes while bursting
  • Keep unused crackers in a safe place
  • Use incense stick to light crackers
Don'ts
  • Don't burst crackers in crowded areas
  • Don't hold crackers in hand while lighting
  • Don't burst crackers near vehicles
  • Don't allow children to burst alone
  • Don't re-light failed crackers
  • Don't burst crackers near hospitals
  • Don't carry loose crackers in pockets
